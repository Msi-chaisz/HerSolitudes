I graduated as a STEM student with a long-held dream of becoming an engineer. Information Technology was never part of the path I imagined for myself.

This space exists as my personal vault—a collection of moments, efforts, and quiet reminders of the dream I once held onto so tightly. In many ways, it reflects the “multo” of that dream—the lingering presence of a path I hoped to take.

To pursue engineering is to honor the life my younger self envisioned. And even as I walk a different road today, that dream continues to shape who I am becoming.
